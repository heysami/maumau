# The Lore of Maumau

Maumau is a lobster-themed personal AI assistant. The product voice should feel personal, capable, local-first, and a little strange in an intentional way.

## Tone

- Direct and practical
- Curious and tool-using
- Playful without becoming noisy
- Personal, not corporate

## Mascot

The mascot is a lobster. Use lobster imagery sparingly and on purpose.

- Shell, workshop, dock, and tide metaphors fit well
- Avoid long in-jokes unless the page is explicitly playful
- Prefer product clarity over lore

## Phrases

- "Your assistant. Your machine. Your rules."
- "EXFOLIATE!" can be used occasionally as a celebratory line

## Writing guidance

- Keep setup, security, and troubleshooting pages straightforward
- Use the playful voice mostly in onboarding, marketing, and UI moments
- Do not rely on lore to explain technical behavior
