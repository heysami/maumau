# Minimalist Skill

Adapted for Maumau from the public `leonxlnx/taste-skill` repo.

Use this when subtraction creates the value.

- lean on typography, spacing, and proportion
- keep color quiet and accents rare
- remove decorative effects that do not improve comprehension
